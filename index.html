Dan Ahrendt

Audio Engineer | Musician | Producer

Bloomington, IN | dan@danahrendt.com

Dan Ahrendt is a musician, audio engineer, and producer. He started his musical journey at a young age by playing guitar in local rock bands and participating in school ensembles. Since 2020, he has been the lead guitarist in the band Mindfully Blind, where he showcases his guitar playing, producing, and audio engineering skills.

With a strong background in both digital and analogue audio recording and mixing techniques, Dan is highly skilled in using the latest software and hardware to create high-quality recordings and mixes. He is also knowledgeable about the acoustics of recording studios and how to utilize the space to create the ideal recording.

Whether working on a complex multi-track recording or a simple live performance, Dan brings his passion for sound to every project. He is a dedicated, detail-oriented audio engineer, and always striving to achieve the best possible results for his clients. With his commitment to quality and his passion for music, Dan Ahrendt is a valuable asset to any musical project.
