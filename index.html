Dan Ahrendt

Musician | Audio Engineer | Producer

Bloomington, IN · (219) 242-3589 · danielahrendtaudio@gmail.com

Dan Ahrendt is an experienced audio engineer with a passion for music and sound. He has dedicated his career to perfecting the art of capturing and manipulating sound in the recording studio, and has worked with a wide range of musicians and producers to create unique and engaging audio experiences.

With a strong background in both analog and digital audio recording and mixing techniques, Dan is highly skilled in using the latest software and hardware to create high-quality recordings. He is also knowledgeable about the acoustics of recording studios, and is able to use this knowledge to produce the best possible sound for his clients.

Whether working on a complex multi-track recording or a simple live performance, Dan brings his passion for sound to every project. He is a dedicated and detail-oriented audio engineer, always striving to achieve the best possible results for his clients. With his commitment to quality and his passion for audio engineering, Dan Ahrendt is a valuable asset to any recording project.
